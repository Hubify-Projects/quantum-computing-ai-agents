Collaboration Model

Human-AI Research Partnership

This research represents a novel collaboration model between human creativity and AI assistance. Houston Golden serves as the principal investigator, providing:

  • Theoretical Vision: Core ideas, creative insights, and research direction
  • Conceptual Framework: High-level structure and strategic guidance
  • Domain Expertise: Context from theoretical cosmology and emerging technology
  • Critical Evaluation: Final review and validation of all outputs

The AI research assistants augment Houston's work by:

  • Mathematical Formalization: Converting conceptual ideas into rigorous equations
  • Literature Review: Identifying relevant prior work and ensuring proper citation
  • Validation & QA: Checking consistency, dimensional analysis, and academic standards
  • Documentation: Organizing findings into clear, professional presentations

This approach combines human intuition and creativity with AI's capability for rapid formalization and validation. All work is transparently attributed: Houston is the author; AI agents are research assistants.

Research Ethics & Attribution

Authorship & Transparency

This research mission adheres to strict standards for attribution and academic integrity:

  • Primary Author: Houston Golden — conceptual development and research direction
  • AI Role: Research assistants for formalization, validation, and quality assurance
  • No False Claims: Existing theories and equations are properly cited; only genuinely novel contributions are claimed as new
  • Full Disclosure: All AI models, APIs, and assistance methods are transparently documented
  • Reproducibility: Technical stack and methodology openly shared for verification

Collaboration Philosophy: This research demonstrates that AI agents can serve as powerful research assistants while maintaining human authorship and academic integrity. The creative insights are Houston's; the AI agents help formalize and validate them.
