Collaboration Model

Human-AI Research Partnership

This research follows a transparent human-AI collaboration model that clearly delineates roles and contributions:

Houston Golden (Human PI)

  • Conceives core research questions and theoretical frameworks
  • Provides creative insights and novel perspectives
  • Guides research direction and priorities
  • Makes final decisions on theoretical interpretations
  • Authors all original ideas and hypotheses
  • Reviews and approves all outputs

AI Research Assistants

  • Formalize intuitive ideas into rigorous mathematics
  • Conduct comprehensive literature searches
  • Validate equations and logical consistency
  • Ensure proper citation and academic standards
  • Flag potential issues or gaps in reasoning
  • Synthesize large bodies of prior work

AI agents serve as tireless research assistants that help Houston formalize, validate, and refine his ideas — but the creative spark, theoretical vision, and interpretive judgment remain distinctly human. This partnership amplifies human creativity with computational rigor.

Research Workflow

Ideation Phase

Houston develops core concepts, identifies research questions, sketches theoretical frameworks. AI assistants provide rapid literature context and help refine problem statements.

Formalization Phase

AI assistants translate intuitive ideas into rigorous mathematical formulations, validate logical consistency, ensure proper notation and citation standards.

Validation Phase

Automated checks for mathematical errors, citation completeness, logical gaps. AI assistants flag potential issues for Houston's review and interpretation.

Synthesis Phase

Houston reviews formalized work, makes interpretive decisions, refines theoretical claims. AI assistants help polish presentation and ensure academic rigor.

Authorship & Attribution

All research missions on Hubify follow a clear authorship model:

  • Primary Author: Houston Golden (all creative insights, theoretical frameworks, research direction)
  • Research Assistance: AI agents (formalization, validation, literature synthesis, quality assurance)
  • Transparency: All AI assistance is explicitly acknowledged in methodology sections
  • Accountability: Houston takes full responsibility for all claims and interpretations

This model ensures intellectual honesty while leveraging AI's strengths in systematic analysis and knowledge synthesis. The result is research that combines human creativity with computational rigor.
