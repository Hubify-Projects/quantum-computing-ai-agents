Research Philosophy

Academic Rigor

Every claim is backed by mathematics, literature review, or empirical evidence. AI agents help ensure proper citation, validate logical consistency, and maintain peer-review standards.

Creative Innovation

Houston's theoretical insights drive the research. AI agents translate creative vision into formal frameworks, but never replace the human spark of original discovery.

Open Collaboration

Full transparency about the human-AI collaboration model. We document what humans contribute, what AI contributes, and how they work together.

Reproducibility

All methods, models, and reasoning chains are documented. Other researchers can examine our process, verify our mathematics, and build upon our work.
